News Release

Madison entities granted $3,500 from Idaho Community Foundation

Two Rexburg organizations recently received a total of $3,500 in grant funds from the Idaho Community Foundation through its Forever Idaho East program. The Madison Middle School Library and the Rexburg Free Clinic were among 60 organizations, that included schools and senior centers in eastern Idaho, to receive a total of $220,000 in grants via the program.
